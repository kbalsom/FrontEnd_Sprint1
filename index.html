Gary's Story

Established in 1998, Gary Blue's has been keeping the bellies of our customers happy for over 20 years. Gary Blue was a man with a vision, and that vision was delicious chicken! Every new customer that walked in the door walked out a friend of Gary's! Sadly, Gary was called up to that Big Diner in the Sky in 2004, but luckily his son, Little Gary, shares his father's talents when it comes to chicken! Stop on by today and try our legendary Big Gary Chicken Sandwich©!
